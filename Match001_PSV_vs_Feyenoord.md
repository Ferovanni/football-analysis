Match Report 1: PSV Eindhoven Season-Week: 2025-2026, Week 10 Opponent: Feyenoord Formation: 4-2-3-1 In Possession: 3-2-5 Out of Possession: 5-2-3 Pressing: High press and high defensive line Transitions: They generally play set-piece offense, but they also have counter-attacking players who can create danger up front when they get the chance. Strengths: Forcing the opponent to defend, creating a chance for a quick counter-attack through high-tempo pressing when the opponent is attacking, and saving time in regaining possession of the ball. Weaknesses: The risk of conceding counter-attacks is high; the full-backs' defensive skills aren't strong enough, and there's a high risk of being caught with too few players when the ball is lost. Interesting Observation: Their insistence on playing out from the back with short passes and their refusal to deviate from this decision even in very risky situations.
